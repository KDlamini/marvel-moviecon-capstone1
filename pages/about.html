"Marvel Celebrates Movies!"

MARVEL CINEMATIC UNIVERSE
PHASE 4 EXPANSION 2021

The MovieCon brings together Marvel Studios president, writers, directors, producers, and actors to discuss revealed new feature films for theaters and series to release on Disney+. Announcements are the next entries from the Disney+ TV slate that has grown to include Armor Wars, Ironheart, Secret Invasion, Hawkeye, Ms. Marvel, Moon Knight, She-Hulk, and What If...?, the first animated series from Marvel Studios. Phases Four and Five of the Marvel Cinematic Universe grows further with a rebooted Fantastic Four from Spider-Man director Jon Watts. All part of the franchise's ongoing expansion, where the television series will interlink with the feature films for the first time, the newly set Disney+ titles join a roster of limited series that includes the already streaming WandaVision, The Falcon and the Winter Soldier, and Loki.
