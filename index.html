"Marvel Celebrates Movies!"

MARVEL CINEMATIC UNIVERSE
PHASE 4 EXPANSION
2021

The Walt Disney Company plans to release more than 30 originals from its Marvel, Lucasfilm, and Disney animation arms over the next several years. The Marvel MovieCon will be an exiting event for Marvel fans to have an open discussion about the next phase of the MCU.

2021-11-26(FRI) ~ 27(SAT)

@ San Diego Convention Center, California, USA

Main Program

  • Actors Panel

  • Sit with your favorite celebrities that will feature in the next movies and ask questions about their experiences.

  • Slate Exhibition

  • Meet with Kevin Feige where he will be revealing new and exciting upcoming content on both the big screen and on Disney+.

  • Forum

  • Have the time to share your thoughts and opinions with movie writers, directors, as well as other fans about what's next in phase 4.

  • Costume Party

  • Dress up as your favourite character and celebrate their heroism with your family and friends in a fun and colorful Marvel theme.
